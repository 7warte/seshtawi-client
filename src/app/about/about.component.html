Design matters

Design is crucial in web development because it directly impacts user experience and engagement. A well-designed frontend ensures intuitive navigation, making it easy for users to find what they need. It also enhances the website’s visual appeal, which can build trust and credibility with users. Good design improves accessibility, ensuring the site is usable for everyone, including those with disabilities. Overall, effective design in web development helps create a positive first impression, encourages interaction, and ultimately drives conversions.
